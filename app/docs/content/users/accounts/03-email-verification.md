## Overview

Spacewave Cloud requires a verified email address before you can use cloud features. After signing up for a Cloud plan, you will be directed to the email verification page if no verified email is on file.

## When Verification Is Needed

You are shown the verification page after checkout when your account has no verified email. The page lists all email addresses associated with your account, each showing whether it is verified or not. You need at least one verified email to proceed.

## Verifying Your Email

1. On the verification page, find your email address in the list and click **Send code**.
2. Check your inbox for a 6-digit verification code.
3. Enter the code in the input field that appears below your email address.
4. Click **Verify email**.
5. Once verified, a green checkmark appears next to the email and a **Continue** button lets you proceed into the app.

## Adding a Different Email

If you want to verify a different address instead, expand the "Use a different email" section at the bottom of the page. Enter the new address and click **Add & send code**. The new email appears in the list, and a verification code is sent automatically.

## Resending the Code

After sending a code, there is a cooldown period before you can resend. The button shows a countdown (e.g., "30s") during this time. Once the timer expires, click **Send code** or the "Didn't get it? Send again" link to request a new code.

## Removing an Unverified Email

You can remove unverified email addresses that are not your primary email by clicking the trash icon next to them. Primary emails cannot be removed.

## Troubleshooting

- **"Invalid or expired code."** The code may have expired. Send a new one and enter it promptly.
- **"Failed to send verification email."** Check that the email address is correctly formatted and try again.
- **Code not arriving.** Check your spam folder. If the cooldown timer is still active, wait for it to expire before requesting another code.
