## Your Data, Your Devices

Most apps keep your data on someone else's server. If that server goes down, or the company changes its terms, or you lose internet, your data becomes inaccessible. You are a guest in someone else's house.

Spacewave flips this around. Your data lives on your own devices. Everything works offline. When your devices are connected, they sync with each other directly. You can optionally use cloud storage as a backup, but even then, your data is encrypted so that only you can read it.

## What You Can Do

Spacewave gives you a set of built-in tools that all share the same private, syncing storage:

- **Drive** for storing and browsing files of any size.
- **Git repositories** for source-code workspaces.
- **Canvas** for visual workspaces.

Everything you create syncs automatically between your linked devices. Upload a file from your desktop, access it from the browser, or keep a project workspace available across devices.

## Key Ideas

A **space** is like a project folder. Each space is its own private container with its own files, settings, and tools. You can have as many spaces as you want.

**Plugins** add new capabilities to a space. When you create a Drive space, the file browser plugin is what makes it work. You can install additional plugins to do more with your spaces.

**Device linking** connects your devices together. Once linked, they share access to all your spaces and keep everything in sync. No manual transfers or cloud accounts required.

## Privacy Built In

Your data is encrypted on your device before it goes anywhere. If you use cloud backup, the server stores scrambled data it cannot read. Your devices talk to each other over encrypted connections. Nobody sitting between your devices can see what you are working on.

## Who is Spacewave For

Spacewave is for anyone who wants to own their data while still getting the convenience of sync and collaboration. If you have ever been frustrated by an app that did not work offline, or worried about a company having access to your private files, Spacewave is built for you.

Developers can also extend Spacewave by building plugins. See the Developer Documentation section for details.
